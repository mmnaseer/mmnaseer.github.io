About

I am a dedicated PhD researcher at Teesside University International Business School, specializing in climate change, corporate social responsibility, and green finance. My research mainly explores how climate risks affect firm value, market volatility, and ESG performance.

Research interests

  • Climate risk exposure and firm outcomes
  • Corporate governance and ethics
  • ESG performance and disclosure

Methods

  • Panel econometrics (FE/RE, quantile)
  • Textual analysis of earnings calls
  • PSM and robustness designs
