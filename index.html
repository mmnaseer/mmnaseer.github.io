I am an applied researcher working at the intersection of finance, economics, and sustainability. My work examines how climate risk, environmental regulation, and institutional quality affect corporate behaviour, financial markets, and firm performance. I specialise in large-scale panel data analysis and causal inference, with a strong interest in policy-relevant research.

Current Research Areas

  • Climate and Transition Risk.
  • Corporate Finance.
  • ESG and Sustainability.
  • Financial Volatility.
  • Governance and Institutions.
