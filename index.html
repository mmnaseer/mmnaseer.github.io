Mirza Muhammad Naseer

I work on corporate governance and sustainability with a focus on climate risk, firm value, ESG performance, and financial volatility. My research uses large-scale international panel data, textual analysis, and causal inference methods.

Current focus
Corporate climate risk exposure and its implications for valuation, ESG, and volatility.
Methods
Panel econometrics (FE/RE, REGHDFE), matching, quantile regression, and text-as-data.

Research

My research investigates how climate risk and corporate governance interact to influence firm value, ESG performance, and market volatility. I also study governance mechanisms related to executive incentives, board composition, corruption risk, and ethical decision-making.
